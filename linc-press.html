About Linc

Linc is a relationship tracker that helps you maintain meaningful connections with the people you care about. With automatic reminders, conversation prompts, and relationship metrics, Linc takes the anxiety out of staying in touch.

Key Features

Streaks & Tracking: Relationship metrics and streak indicators show how good of a job you're doing at staying in touch

Easy Conversation: Curated talking prompts that help get the conversation flowing for use in iMessages, SMS, WhatsApp, Signal, and more

Persistent Reminders: Apple Reminders integration and in-app persistent notifications continue to remind you until you take action

Sync & Backup: iCloud sync ensures that all of your relationships sync between your iPhone, iPad, and Vision Pro

Pricing

Linc is available as a free download with premium features available through subscription.

Platform Availability

iPhone, iPad, and Vision Pro
